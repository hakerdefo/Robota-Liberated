## 🌿 Philosophy

The word *robota* — Czech for drudgery and forced labour — is the root from which Karel Čapek conjured the word *robot* in 1920. Every Android device arrives shackled: a robot built to serve its manufacturer, its carrier, its advertiser. This list exists to change that — one open-source app at a time.

Every application here is:

- ✅ **Free & Open Source** — inspect it, fork it, trust it
- ✅ **Available via F-Droid, IzzyOnDroid, or project's own F-Droid repo** — no Google Play Store required
- ✅ **Selected on merit** — the finest tool available for each purpose
- ✅ **Respectful of your privacy** — no dark patterns, no telemetry, no surveillance

## 🤝 Contributing

Contributions are warmly welcomed. If you know of an app that:

- Is free and open source
- Is available on F-Droid, IzzyOnDroid, or project's own F-Droid repo
- Fills a genuine gap or is demonstrably superior to an existing entry

Please open an **Issue** to discuss it or submit a **Pull Request** directly. All contributions are reviewed for quality, philosophy, and fit.

We've kept one app per category with intentional exceptions. **Browser**: Two engines, three philosophies. Fennec brings Firefox's extension ecosystem and tight privacy controls; Cromite offers a hardened Chromium alternative for those who need or prefer that engine; and IronFox is for those who prefer security and privacy above anything and are willing to investigate occasional site breaks. **Email**: Three distinct paradigms. Delta Chat reimagines email as decentralized instant messaging. FairEmail is a minimal, privacy-obsessed, fully featured client for traditional email management. Thunderbird is the powerhouse — feature-rich and built for those who want a complete communication hub.

**Intentionally excluded**: Messaging, social media, and gaming apps. There are simply too many excellent options to fairly represent, and this list isn't designed to be a be-all, end-all list of everything open source, but if you are curious, we suggest you try **[Session](https://fdroid.getsession.org/)** or **[SimpleX Chat](https://f-droid.org/en/packages/chat.simplex.app/)** for messaging and **[Mastodon](https://f-droid.org/en/packages/org.joinmastodon.android/)** or **[Bluesky](https://bsky.app/)** for social media. Honestly, our knowledge of games is ridiculously limited, but why don't you try **[Lichess](https://f-droid.org/en/packages/org.lichess.mobileV2/)**, **[Pixel Wheels](https://f-droid.org/en/packages/com.agateau.tinywheels.android/)**, or **[Unciv](https://f-droid.org/en/packages/com.unciv.app/)**?
